Twippe App

Twippe is a project created by Fabian Perez at Nuclio Digital School during the master in Digital Product Management in Barcelona.

This project was born from my passion about cycling and technology and also a pain that I was suffering as an amateur cyclist (user) while living in Barcelona.

Starts with describing a context of the sitation as well as the opportunity the there is in the current market validate it from a big competir called Strava.

Context

Mission, vision and values were created during and everything goes around them in project: Community, Balance, Creativity, Closeness, Passion. You can check more about it in the website

Our mission is to create a community of cycling making it more interactive, fun and challenging through technology.

Once we defined our integrity as a product, we establish our market target (for sure in Barcelona - we can set a call and I will give the reason). Also creating a Value Proposition Canvas with the "customer jobs", "pains", "gains", etc. With this also huge and deep Benchmark was done about the main competitors : Strava, Runtastic, Koomot and others, The research was about their business model, payments, monetization, marketing, which kind of maps they use, amount of users, ratings in the stores, etc.

For our project at the beginning only User Persona was defined, but at the end of our first iteration we ended up with 3 different User Personas.

Product Strategy

In this part of the project a Business Canvas Model was created, also a Unique Selling Proposition that was refined several times meanwhile we learned more about our users and identify other possibles differentiations between the apps in the current market.

MVP

MVP Pre-product

This stage of the product discovery includes a logo creation, website, an instagram profile and a survey using Google Forms where we receive more than 300 answers and very useful data validating a lot of our hipotesis and also learning about others scenarios. (Fail fast - Learn fast). For this a total of 10 euros were spend in an instagram campaing.

Marketing & Adquisition

Besides the justifying of our digital channels to create our go to market campaing, we added a model of market prenetration using influencers and popular cyclist around Spain and then scaling the model to Europe and other continents.
